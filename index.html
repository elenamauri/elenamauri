About Me

I work as a UX/UI Designer @ Boraso while also working as a Freelance UX/UI Designer. Before my current role, I had the opportunity to work as Innovation Intern in the field of generative AI and as UI Designer at Bticino.
I hold a Master's degree in Human–Computer Interaction from the University of Trento where I graduated with a thesis on Brain–Computer Interfaces. My academic journey equipped me with a profound understanding of user-centered design principles, cognitive psychology, and interaction design, forming a strong foundation for my career in UX/UI and AI innovation. I am also exploring the field of Human Factors Engineering.
